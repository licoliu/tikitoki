          General options

          Timeline controls

          The timeline controls allow timeline visitors to search, filter and zoom your timeline, as well as change the view display and spacing options.

          Lazy image loading

          Enabling lazy image loading helps your timeline load faster by only loading images when they are visible on screen. Recommended if your timeline has lots of images.

          HTML Formatting

          Enable HTML formatting in a story's full text field (see the Extra info tab) and the timeline's about field.

          Intro Panel

          Do you want to show the intro panel explaining what the timeline is about when people first arrive on the timeline page.

          Read more links

          Choose if you want the 'Read more' buttons and story links to open in a new window or not.

          Url date hash

          Choose to enable or disable the date/story hash that appears in the url and saves your positon on the timeline when you reload.

          Make timeline copyable

          Choose whether you want other people to be able to copy your timeline. If yes, a "Copy this timeline" button will appear on your timeline.
